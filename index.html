About ME

  

Hi there, I'm Jackeline. I'm a graphic Designer, Illustrator and Digital Artist. My work emphasizes on being neat, and calming. Most of my art pieces convey a feeling of calmness and peace and enjoyment. I use soft and warm colors most of the time so that I can transmit that peace. It's very important to me that every piece of art is well detailed.

I graduated from LaGuardia Community College majoring in New Media Technology.  Through this major I learned more skills, not only in graphic design, but in web design, video editing, and  programming(HTML,CSS) as well.
